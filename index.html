GYM FACILITIES

Motivation is what gets you started;

habit is what keeps you going.

An inspired workout requires inspirational equipment, which is why we utilise the latest FreeMotion® training technology—the equipment of choice for many of the world’s leading athletes. FreeMotion® gym equipment adapts itself to your movements, allowing you to perform a variety of exercises using a single machine. Get the most from your workout and target specific areas or muscle groups without changing settings.

Feed your fitness appetite with access to workouts and global marathons powered by Google Maps™. Let the FreeMotion® iFit® running machines automatically adjust to simulate the terrain as you follow the Google Street View route on your digital display. Test your limits with our rowing machines, cross trainers, free weights and spinning bikes, whilst being motivated by your own soundtrack on our Internet-enabled entertainment systems.

PERSONAL TRAINING

Strive for progress, not perfection. If it doesn’t challenge you, it doesn’t change you.

Our inspirational personal trainer, Mark, will work with you to create a bespoke training programme that focuses on achieving your health and fitness goals. With over 26 years experience working within the fitness training industry, including 12 years coaching professional boxing athletes, he lives and breathes a passion for personal training.

Whether your target is weight loss, muscle gain or simply improving your overall fitness levels, Mark will provide the support and advice you need to take control of your well being. He will work with you on a one-to-one basis or as a group to help you develop and maintain an all-round fitness and lifestyle plan.
